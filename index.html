I. Introduction and Project Description¶

The world of retail is rich in data and ripe for analytical exploration. One food item that has captured the world's attention in recent years is the avocado. This unassuming fruit has escalated into a cultural phenomenon, attracting a diverse array of stakeholders from fitness aficionados and culinary experts to economists and market strategists. The avocado's meteoric rise can be attributed to a myriad of factors, including its price volatility, distinct seasonality, and the surge in consumer demand driven by health trends. In a society that is becoming increasingly health-conscious, understanding the dynamics of the avocado market isn't just an academic exercise; it holds tangible ramifications for producers, retailers, and consumers.

For this project, I have found a dataset that encapsulates weekly retail scan data on the volume and pricing of Hass avocados, spanning from 2015 through 2018. Comprising thirteen variables, the dataset offers a panoramic overview of the market dynamics specific to Hass avocados. Among the variables included are the average price, total sales volume, a breakdown of packaging types signified by various bag sizes, and the geographic regions where these sales occurred. This rich array of data dimensions enables a multi-faceted exploration into the complexities of the avocado market.

I found this dataset on Kaggle. The person that posted the dataset on Kaggle retrieved the data from the Hass Avocado Board website.

I.a. Project Goals¶

For this project, I will be working alone and exploring the following questions:

Basic EDA Questions

  • How do avocado prices fluctuate over time?
  • Can we identify seasonal trends in avocado sales?
  • What cities have the highest and lowest sales volumes?

More Complicated Questions by Category

Time-Series Analysis

  • Seasonal Decomposition: Can we decompose the time series data to identify the seasonal, trend, and residual components of avocado prices?
  • Autocorrelation: Is there any autocorrelation in the time series of avocado prices, and if so, what could it imply?

Feature Engineering and Analysis

  • Price Elasticity: Can we calculate the price elasticity of demand for avocados? This will require creating new variables.
  • Bag Preference: Is there a preference for certain types of bags (Small Bags, Large Bags, XLarge Bags) over time or across regions?

Regional Analysis

  • Price vs. Volume by Region: Is there an inverse relationship between price and sales volume across different regions?
  • Hotspot Identification: What are the 'hotspots' for avocado sales based on a combination of high volume and high price?

Type-Based Analysis

  • Conventional vs. Organic: How do the sales and prices of conventional avocados compare with organic ones?
  • Price Premium for Organic: Is there a consistent 'price premium' for organic avocados over conventional ones, and how does this vary by region and time?

Machine Learning

  • Price Prediction: Can we build a machine learning model to predict future avocado prices based on past data?
  • Clustering Regions: Can machine learning clustering techniques like K-means identify regions with similar avocado buying behaviors?

Statistical Tests

  • Hypothesis Testing: Is there a statistically significant difference in the average price of avocados between different years?
  • Correlation Analysis: Are any of the numerical variables in the dataset significantly correlated?

Complexity through Combination

  • Multi-level Analysis: Can we create a multi-level model that considers both time and region as hierarchical levels for modeling avocado prices? What might this reveal?
  • Interaction Effects: Are there interaction effects between variables like type and region on the price?

Anomaly Detection

  • Outlier Detection: Can we identify and explain any outliers in avocado prices or sales volumes? Are these due to specific events or anomalies in data collection? To answer this question, I may need to cross-analyze this Hass avocado dataset with another dataset (e.g. one that contains time-stamped historical events).
